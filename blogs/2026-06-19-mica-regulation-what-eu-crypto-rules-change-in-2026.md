MiCA's transitional period ends July 1, 2026, after which any crypto-asset service provider without a CASP authorisation cannot legally serve EU clients. Licensed providers become obliged entities under EU AML rules, requiring identity verification at onboarding, transaction monitoring, and sanctions and PEP screening, while stablecoins face full-backing and no-interest constraints.
