Address verification confirms that a customer's declared residential or business address is real, belongs to them, and was collected to satisfy the regulator's documentation standard. The article shows how fintechs and neobanks balance the FinCEN, FCA, and FINTRAC frameworks with low-friction onboarding using doc-less electronic verification and document-based proof of address.
